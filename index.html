I am most interested in Fullstack Web Development and User Experience. Recently, I have really enjoyed working with React and Sass and learning more about how to design good websites. In academic research, I like to tinker with my hands and think about how computer technology affects the customer. Building small prototypes from scratch and getting user feedback is fun, which is why MIT's HCIE lab group was an a great fit.

While I am still searching for the best way I can make an impact on the world, I am most motivated when I feel like an important contributor to the team and can directly help make someone happier, whether it be a customer or a fellow coworker. Eager to take on tasks that require learning about new topics, I have been described as an easy person to get along with and consistently a benefit to a team.
